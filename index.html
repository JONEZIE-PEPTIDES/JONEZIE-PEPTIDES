FAQ

Clear answers do more work than inflated claims.

What makes Jonezie feel different from a typical peptide site?

The goal is a cleaner buy flow, stronger product detail, easier option selection, and a catalog that feels deliberate instead of recycled.

What should a strong Jonezie product page include?

Each page should explain the compound category, summarize the research angle, show the live MG options, and make the research-use boundary obvious.

How should buyers think about quality and confidence?

The strongest peptide brands do not just throw around purity language. They make the catalog readable, label options clearly, and give buyers enough information to compare products intelligently.

How are kit offers supposed to work here?

If a product gets a full kit offer, Jonezie should present that option as 8 vials instead of 10 so the store keeps its own distinct structure.

Can the site keep improving as more assets come in?

Yes. The structure is already set up to keep absorbing more product images, stronger product copy, merch drops, category organization, and credibility content without rebuilding the whole site again.
