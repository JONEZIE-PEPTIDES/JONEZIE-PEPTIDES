Why Jonezie

Built by a buyer who got tired of hype pricing and half-truths.

I started where most people start: curious, confused, and trying to figure out who to trust. After buying from major peptide brands, I realized the quality conversation and the pricing conversation were not always the same thing.

I started digging deeper into how this industry actually works. Testing, sourcing, supplier relationships, and markup structure quickly became an obsession.

What I found was frustrating: too many brands act like manufacturers when they are mostly middlemen with premium branding and premium pricing.

So I cut out the noise, went direct to reliable producers, and built supplier relationships focused on consistent quality and cleaner research standards.

Then the mission got simple: find the purest peptides possible and make them available without the insane markup.

Most companies mark products up 1,500% to 2,000% or more. I do not. I take on the risk, shipping friction, and backend headaches so buyers can get fairer pricing.

Mission

Pure product. Fair pricing. No fake story.

  • Direct sourcing relationships focused on research quality.
  • Transparent pricing philosophy without inflated brand-tax markup.
  • A cleaner buying experience that respects serious researchers.

I am still building, still learning, and still pushing. But I will always keep this simple: tell the truth, protect the buyer, and keep the fire research alive.
