JONEZIE LABS RUO QUICK REFERENCE

Research Use Only. Not for human or veterinary use.

STORAGE
- Store unopened lyophilized vials in a cool, dry, light-protected environment.
- After reconstitution, refrigerate promptly.
- Minimize repeated warm-to-cold cycles.
- Label mix dates and keep lot-level notes when comparing products.

RECONSTITUTION
- Concentration = compound amount (mg) / dilution volume (mL)
- Example: 10mg with 2mL dilution = 5mg/mL
- Add diluent slowly along the vial wall.
- Swirl gently instead of shaking aggressively.

MIXING WORKFLOW
- Confirm the correct vial label, strength, and target dilution volume first.
- Keep handling surfaces clean and session notes consistent.
- Refrigerate after reconstitution and document timing.
